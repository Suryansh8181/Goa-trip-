🏖 Pegasus Team Goa Getaway 🌴

Trip for Pegasus Team | September 13th - 15th, 2025

Whispering Palms Beach Resort, Candolim

Heads Up!

September marks the end of the monsoon season. Expect lush green views and fewer crowds, but keep an eye out for occasional rain. Seasonal markets like Anjuna and Arpora will be closed during this time.

🛫 Day 1: Arrival & Candolim Exploration

Saturday, 13th September 2025

⏰ Morning

  • ✈️ Flight: Indigo flight from HYD at 07:35 AM, arriving at GOX at 08:50 AM.
  • 🚕 Transfer: Pre-book a tempo traveller or two taxis for the 1.5-hour drive to the resort.

🍽 Afternoon

  • 🍛 Lunch: Enjoy authentic Goan food at **Feni Restaurant and Bar**.
  • 🏖️ Relax: Unwind at the peaceful Candolim Beach.

🌆 Evening

  • 🍹 Dinner & Drinks: Experience beachside vibes at **L'Amore Cafe & Restaurant**.

🚗 Day 2: North Goa Adventures

Sunday, 14th September 2025

🏕 Morning

  • 🚗 Travel: Hire a tempo traveller or two SUVs for the day.
  • 🏰 Visit: Explore the historic **Fort Aguada** for stunning sea views.

🎮 Afternoon

  • 🧈 Lunch: Head to Vagator for lunch at the popular **Thalassa** (reservations are highly recommended).
  • 🛍️ Shopping: As markets are closed, explore local boutiques in the Candolim-Calangute area.

🌇 Evening

  • 🌅 Sunset: Watch the sunset from the iconic **Chapora Fort**.
  • 🍴 Dinner: Enjoy dinner at one of the vibrant restaurants in Vagator or Anjuna.

🌅 Day 3: Leisure & Departure

Monday, 15th September 2025

⛱ Morning

  • 🏊 Relax: Spend your final hours by the pool or on Candolim Beach.
  • 🕌 Optional: Visit the churches in Old Goa for a cultural experience.

🏄 Lunch & Departure

  • 🍲 Lunch: Have a final meal at **Infantaria Cafe** in Calangute.
  • 🚗 Airport Transfer: Depart for Manohar International Airport (GOX) for your flights home.
